Kanha Makeup Studio

At Kanha Makeup Studio and Academy, we believe that beauty is an art, and every face is a unique canvas. Founded and led by internationally certified makeup artist Madhvi, our studio is located in the heart of Jalandhar, India. With a passion for transforming looks and enhancing natural beauty, Madhvi and her team are dedicated to providing the highest level of professional makeup services. Our studio offers a wide range of services, from bridal makeup to everyday glam, ensuring that every client leaves feeling confident and radiant. As a hub for beauty enthusiasts, we also run a makeup academy where aspiring makeup artists can learn and hone their skills under expert guidance.
